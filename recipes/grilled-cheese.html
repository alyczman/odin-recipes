Grilled Cheese Sandwich

How to Make a Grilled Cheese

Everyone needs to know how to make a classic grilled cheese sandwich. Whether you're a beginner cook or an old pro, you'll come back to this top-rated grilled cheese recipe again and again.

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of white break
  • 3 tablespoons of butter
  • two slices of cheddar cheese

Directions

  1. Preheat a nonstick skillet over a medium heat. Generously butter one side of bread and place butter side down
  2. Add one slice of cheese. Butter another slice of bread and place on top of the cheese butter side up
  3. Cook until browned on one side. Flip and continue cooking until cheese is melted.
  4. Repeat with remaining slides of bread and cheese.
