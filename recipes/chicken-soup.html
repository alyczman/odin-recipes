Classic Chicken Noodle Soup

The Best Soup for your Soul

Chicken noodle soup has been been one of my favorite meals since I was a little kid. Whether you're just coming in from a day of playing outside in the snow or just having an off day, this is a meal guaranteed to pick up your spirits. It can be as complex or simple as you want or need - the key, however, is to use fresh ingredients to ensure you're getting its full benefits.

Ingredients

  • Whole Chicken
  • 1 White Onion, peeled
  • 4-5 Large Carrots, peeled
  • 4-5 Celery Hearts
  • 2 Garlic cloves, peeled
  • 1 Tablespoon Thyme
  • Egg Noodles, half a bag
  • Chicken Boullion Cubes, 2
  • Salt and Pepper (to taste)
